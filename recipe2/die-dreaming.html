Morir So単ando drink

The Morir So単ando Drink, which translates to Die Dreaming is a popular Dominican drink, We drink this for breakfast, for lunch with food, aperitif and for dinner. This is a drink that you'll see everywhere you go in the Dominican Republic, an also here in the United States.

This drink has become so popular around the world that even other Caribbean countries have started to copy this drink. It's refreshing, inexpensive to make, and tastes like heaven on earth. It's soooo good! And It's only 4 to 5 ingredients to make this delicious drink.

Ingredients

  • Fresh oranges or 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
  • 4 - 5 Ice cubes

Prepare

  1. Add orange

    In a container add the orange juice, after squeezing then or opening the juice bottle etc.

  2. Add Ice cubes

    Okay nice now add the ice cubes (make sure you have space for the milk.)

  3. Add milk and vanilla

    (Note: Before adding milk and vanilla, this step is important. If the juice is not cold the milk will go bad and it won't work.)

    Great now that you make sure that the juice is very cold, add the 1 (12oz)can evaporated milk and the 1 tsp vanilla to the juice, mixed until it has blend well together.

  4. Add the Sugar

    Last step now add sugar to the mix for the tasted.

    (Note: too much sugar is bad for your health, avoid this step if you have high diabetes, for your own health it'll taste good without sugar as well.)

Now enjoy this beautiful drink

Now that the juice is made you can have a side if you like to and enjoy it. like we always say in my country. Buen Provecho
