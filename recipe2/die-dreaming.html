Morir Soñando drink

The Morir Soñando Drink, which translates to Die Dreaming is a popular Dominican drink, We drink this for breakfast, for lunch with food, aperitif and for dinner. This is a drink that you'll see everywhere you go in the Dominican Republic, an also here in the United States.

This drink has become so popular around the world that even other Caribbean countries have started to copy this drink. It's refreshing, inexpensive to make, and tastes like heaven on earth. It's soooo good! And It's only 4 to 5 ingredients to make this delicious drink.

ingredients

  • Fresh oranges or 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Sugar
  • 1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
  • Ice cubes

How to prepare

  1. Add orange

    In a container add the 1 cup or orange juice from the container or from the fresh orange. (after squeezing then)

  2. Add Ice cubes

    Okay nice now add the ice cubes make sure you have space for the milk.

  3. Add 1 (12oz) can evaporated milk and the 1 tsp of vanilla

    Great now make sure that the orange juice is very cold,

    (Note: this step is important, if the juice is not cold the milk will go bad and it won't work.)

    Now add the 1 (12oz)can evaporated milk and the 1 tsp vanilla to the juice. mixed until the blend well together.

  4. Add the Sugar

    Last step now add sugar to the mix as much as you like for the tasted. (Note: too much sugar is bad for your heath, avoid this step if you have hight diabetes, for your own health it'll taste good without sugar as well.)

Now Enjoy this beautiful drink

Now that the juice is made you can have a side if you like to and enjoy it. like we always say in my country. Buen Provecho
