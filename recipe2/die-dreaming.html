Die Dreaming

Morir Soñando drink

The Morir Soñando Drink, which translates to Die Dreaming is a popular Dominican drink, We drink this for breakfast, for lunch with food, aperitif and for dinner. This is a drink that you'll see everywhere you go in the Dominican Republic, an also here in the United States.

This drink has become so popular around the world that even other Caribbean countries have started to copy this drink. It's refreshing, inexpensive to make, and tastes like heaven on earth. It's soooo good! And It's only 4 to 5 ingredients to make this delicious drink.

ingredients

  • Fresh oranges or 1 cup of range juice
  • 1ts Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • 1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
  • Ice cubes
